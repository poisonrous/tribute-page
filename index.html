Perseus Jackson

Savior of Olympus

Perseus "Percy" Jackson was born on August 18th, to Poseidon, the Greek god of the seas, storms, earthquakes, and the creator of horses, and Sally Jackson, a mortal who could see through the Mist. He was named after the famous Greek hero Perseus by his mother for good luck, because his namesake was one of the few heroes who had a happy ending and died a peaceful death.

He's saved the Olympus at least twice:

  • The Titan War: he took a leadership role against the titan Kronos and his army, and finally made the crucial decision to trust Luke Castellan with the knife that led to end the war.
  • The Giant War: he was a part of the seven demigods in the prophecy and directly defeated two giants.

He's fight in numerous battles and his kill count sums up to almost 6,000 monsters and demigods killed.

He's currently dating Annabeth Chase.
